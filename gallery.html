Frequently asked questions

01
What is Nestling Woods by Resero?

Nestling Woods by Resero is a premium residential project in Kasauli, offering fully furnished studio and 1 BHK apartments designed as perfect second homes or lucrative investment opportunities.

02
Where is Nestling Woods located?

The project is strategically located on the KumarHatti-Nahan Road, Kasauli, very close to the Chandigarh-Shimla Highway, offering spectacular valley views.

03
What types of apartments are available at Nestling Woods?

We offer fully furnished Studio Apartments and 1 BHK apartments.

04
Is the Nestling Woods project RERA approved?

Yes, Nestling Woods is a RERA-approved project. You can find our RERA registration details [ Rera No: HPRERASOL2024097/P].

05
Is bank loan facility available for purchasing a unit at Nestling Woods?

Yes, bank loan facilities are available from leading nationalized and private banks. Please contact our sales team for more details

06
What is the expected time for possession of the apartments?

The expected offer of possession for the apartments is March 2027.

07
What are the key amenities offered at Nestling Woods?

Nestling Woods boasts a super luxury clubhouse spanning 12,000 sq.ft with amenities like a heated swimming pool, gymnasium, spa, indoor games, community panoramic hill views, kids play area, meditation center, and 24/7 power backup & security.

08
Are the apartments fully furnished?

Yes, all studio and 1 BHK apartments at Nestling Woods are offered fully furnished with high-quality fixtures and fittings.

09
Who will manage the property post-possession?

The units at Nestling Woods will be run and managed by the renowned Stonewood Hotels Group.

10
What is the assured rental income scheme offered?

Post-possession, owners can opt for our assured rental income scheme: ₹25,000 per month for Studio Apartments and ₹32,000 per month for 1 BHK Apartments (terms and conditions apply).

11
What is the benefit of the Stonewood Hotels partnership?

As an owner at Nestling Woods, you'll receive 15 days of complimentary stay per year at any Stonewood Hotels property across India. Additionally, their professional management ensures hassle-free ownership and potential for strong rental returns

12
Can I use my apartment for self-use if I opt for the rental program?

Yes, even if you opt for the lease model post-possession, you will still have the flexibility for self-use, allowing you to enjoy your leisure home whenever you desire.

13
Can I schedule a site visit?

Yes, we would be happy to arrange a site visit for you to experience the beauty and luxury of Nestling Woods firsthand. Please contact our sales team to schedule your visit
